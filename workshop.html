Workshops

At AUM Yoga Studio, we offer different workshops to provide a Unique experience of Yoga and beyond.

Some special teachers/guests and also our studio teachers lead these workshops in wide variety of special interests such as pranayama techniques, about food habits and today's most bothering subject in adults and children alike- Anxiety and kids programs etc.

However, the spaces are very limited and often sold out. So please order online or at the Studio when you spot one you like.

Note: All workshops require pre-registrations and have different durations.
