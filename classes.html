Aum Yoga Studio Classes

At Aum Yoga Studio, we believe that "With Yoga not only should body become flexible, Your mind and emotions, and above all your conciousness should become flexible"

Here we practice more on flexibility and move up from there to achieve more inner well being. And we also believe that yoga should be available for everyone with experienced and reliable gurus.

So Come, get to know the joyous you, become flexible and enhance your inner well being & ultimatelywork on aligning yourslef with the cosmic geometry!!
